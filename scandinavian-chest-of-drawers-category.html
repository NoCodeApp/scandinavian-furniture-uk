What are Scandinavian chest of drawers good for?

Scandinavian console tables are great minimalist items of furniture ideal to decorate a wall in your home. 

They are popular with their minimalist appearance and versatile when it comes to styling.

Scandi chest of drawers are the ideal landing place for keys, wallets, handbags and mail.

A wooden scandinavian console table is a great way to display artwork while optimising space.

 

How much do Scandi chest of drawers cost?

Chest of Drawers in Scandinavian style cost between £600 and £800 for a four and six drawer chest of drawers.

 

How many drawers do chest of drawers have?

Scandi chest of drawers can have 4, 6, 8 and 10 drawers. The more drawers the more space you have but also they tend to cost more.

 

What colours are scandi chest of drawers available in?

Scandi chest of drawers are available in several colours - oak, grey, white, cream and neutral colours.

 

Where can you place scandi chest of drawers?

Scandinavian chests of drawers typically store folded clothing in a bedroom. You can also use a low chest of drawers as a bedside table. 

They are also sometimes used behind the sofa. 

 

What to store in a Scandinavian chest of drawers?

Scandi chest of drawers provide an excellent place to store T-shirts, tank tops, socks, and other articles of clothing. 

Sometimes you can be used to pack away items you don't wear normally or again.

 

Can I use Scandinavian chest of drawers in living room?

Yes, you can, a scandi chest of drawers for your living room is a good living room furniture choice and great for a modern style living room. They are very stylish and sleek, plus they provide a lot of storage space with plenty drawers combinations.
