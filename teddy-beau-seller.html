Teddy Beau

If you are looking for Scandinavian lamp tables, dressing tables, dining chairs, dressing tables, beds, coffee tables, console tables and chest of drawers, then check out Teddy Beau online store.

Compare Now Learn more

Teddy Beau is a stockist of Scandinavian style furniture, based in Stafford in the UK.

 

How can I contact Teddy Beau?

You can contact them by post at: 

Teddybeau, MOOT HQ, Paton Drive, Tollgate Industrial Estate, Stafford, ST16 3EF.

Or by telephone: 0808 164 9070

Alternatively you can also send them an email directly at info@teddybeau.com

 

What payment methods does Teddy Beau accept?

Teddy Beau accepts several payment methods including AMEX, Apple Pay, Dinners, Discover, Google Pay, Maestro, Mastercard, PayPal, Shopify Pay and Visa.

 

What types of delivery methods does Teddy Beau have?

Teddy Beau offers three delivery methods:

  1. Standard UK Delivery
  2. White Glove 2 Man Delivery
  3. Delivery by Teddybeau's approved manufacturers

Your delivery method will vary depending on the items in your basket; by size, weight, fragility and supplier. If you’re unsure of the delivery method of your order please contact Teddy Beau team at info@teddybeau.com. Your delivery estimate will also be shown on your order confirmation.

 

How much is the Standard Delivery cost at Teddy Beau?

Teddy Beau charges for standard UK delivery is £6.95 and free on orders over £100.

 

What is Teddy Beau's returns policy?

You can return your puchase to Teddy Beau for a full refund or exchange within 30 days of delivery. Teddy Beau must be made aware of your intent to return within 14 days of delivery though. Any orders that are cancelled after dispatch will have the shipping costs deducted from the refund.

See more at here: https://teddybeau.com/pages/delivery-returns
