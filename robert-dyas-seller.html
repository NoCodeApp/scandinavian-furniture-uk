Robert Dyas

Find scandi style bedroom, dining and living room furniture such as coffee tables, sideboards, dining chairs, dining tables and dressing tables on Robert Dyas.

Compare Now Learn more

Robert Dyas is a stockist of Scandinavian style furniture, with the head office based in London in the UK. They have 92 stores all over the United Kingdom.

 

How can I contact Robert Dyas?

You can contact them by post at their registered address: 

Robert Dyas Holdings Limited, 1 St Georges Road, Wimbledon, London, United Kingdom, SW19 4DR

Or by telephone: 0333 103 6677

Alternatively you can also send them an email directly at customerservices@robertdyas.co.uk

 

What payment methods does Robert Dyas accept?

Robert Dyas accepts several payment methods including Visa, Visa Debit, AMEX, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, PayPal, Klarna and Visa.

 

What types of delivery methods does Robert Dyas have?

Robert Dyas offers four delivery methods:

  1. Mainland UK (ex Isle of Wight)
  2. Scottish Highlands & Scottish Isles
  3. Isle of Man, Isles of Scilly and Isle of Wight
  4. Northern Ireland

Standard delivery to most of UK Mainland is FREE on orders over £50 from Robert Dyas, and is available on Standard and Bulky products.  This offer excludes items for delivery to Scottish Highlands, Scottish Isles, Isle of Man, Isles of Scilly and Isle of Wight.

Scottish Highlands & Islands postcodes: AB, FK, HS, IV, KA, KW, PA, PH, ZE, LL58-LL78, IM, TR, PO30-41 

 

How much is the Standard Delivery cost at Robert Dyas?

Robert Dyas charges for standard UK delivery is £4.95 to Mainland UK (excluding Isle of Wight) and free on orders over £50.

 

What is Robert Dyas's returns policy?

According to Robert Dyas, in the UK you have a legal right, under the Consumer Contracts Regulations June 2014, to cancel your order within fourteen (14) days following your receipt of the goods. Refunds for orders cancelled under the provisions of the Consumer Contracts Regulations will be processed in accordance with your legal rights.

See more at here: https://www.robertdyas.co.uk/delivery-and-returns
