What are your data protection rights?

Scandinavian Furniture UK would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights.

Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request Scandinavian Furniture UK for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request that Scandinavian Furniture UK correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request Scandinavian Furniture UK to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request that Scandinavian Furniture UK erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that Scandinavian Furniture UK restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object processing - You have the right to object to Scandinavian Furniture UK's processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request that Scandinavian Furniture UK transfer the data that we have collected to another organisation, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us via email on info@staall.com.

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality - Scandinavian Furniture UK uses these cookies do that we recognise you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. A mix of first-party and third-party cookies are used.
  • Advertising - Scandinavian Furniture UK uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. Scandinavian Furniture UK sometimes shares some limited aspects of this data with third parties for advertising purposes. We may also share online data collected through cookies with our advertising partners. This means that when you visit another website, you may be shown advertising based on your browsing patterns on our website.
  • Analytics - Scandinavian Furniture UK would like to set Google Analytics cookies to help us to improve our website by collecting and reporting information on how you use it. For more information on how these cookies work please see our 'Cookies page'. The cookies collect information in an anonymous form.
