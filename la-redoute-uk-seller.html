La Redoute UK

La Redoute UK stocks scandinavian furniture including bedside tables, beds, console tables, desks, coffee tables, bed drawers.

Compare Now Learn more

La Redoute UK is a stockist of Scandinavian style furniture, with the head office based in Glasgow, Scotland in the UK.

What payment methods does LA Redoute UK accept?

LA Redoute UK accept all major debit and credit cards such as Visa, MasterCard, American Express and other payments methods such as PayPal.

 

What types of delivery methods does LA Redoute UK have?

LA Redoute UK offers several delivery methods:

Click & Collect with ParcelShop

FREE on all orders over £60, or £2.99 if you spend less.

Home Delivery

Charge: £3.99

Bulky Item Delivery

Charge: £9.99

 

How much is the Standard Delivery cost at LA Redoute UK?

LA Redoute UK charges for standard UK home delivery £3.99 to Mainland UK.

 

What is LA Redoute UK's returns policy?

LA Redoute UK offer a 21 day returns or exchanges policy under which they’ll refund or replace most goods if returned to them within 21 days of receipt and they are complete, in their original packaging, unused and with any security stickers, seals or tags intact. Returns within our policy are free.
